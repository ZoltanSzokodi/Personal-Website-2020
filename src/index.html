A bit about myself

Casual intro

My name is Zoltan and I am a junior full-stack developer living in Berlin. Thank you for your interest in my work. The projects showcased in this portfolio are my most recent ones. I am constantly working on new ones to improve my coding skills.
It's been a long jurney since I moved from Budapest to Berlin in 2015 searching for new horizons. Quite a few milestones behind me and surely many more to come. Still one of the most important ones came in 2018. Yep, just like that, out of the blue came the great idea - learn Web Development. I mean how could I go wrong with IT, right?
Frankly it's been a long fun and frustrating rewarding process. From tutorial purgatory to actually building projects on my own and finally reaching the day when I deploy my first portfolio! Cheers!
